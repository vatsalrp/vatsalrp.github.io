Differences 3D

Info: Spot the Difference Game | Unity | C#

Description: Differences 3D is a 3D spot-the-difference game where players are presented with two nearly identical 3D scenes and must identify subtle differences between them. The game features a variety of levels with increasing complexity, requiring attention to detail and strong visual recognition skills. It includes a increasing challenges to increase engagement and provide a sense of difficulty.

Hints are available to assist players when they struggle. The game tracks player performance and progression to create a balanced and rewarding experience.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed core gameplay logic, including difference detection and validation.
  • Created multiple 3D levels with balanced difficulty progression.
  • Designed and implemented the user interface, including hint and score systems.
  • Ensured smooth camera controls and player interaction in 3D space.
