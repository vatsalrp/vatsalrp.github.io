Ved: Journey Beyond The Cube

Info: 3D Puzzle-Adventure Game | Unity | C#

Description: Ved is a 3D puzzle game that challenges players to manipulate objects and solve intricate puzzles to progress through levels. The game focuses on logic-based challenges, requiring players to think critically and interact with the environment in meaningful ways. It features a clean and minimalistic art style, enhancing clarity and focus on puzzle-solving.

Each level is designed to introduce new mechanics, gradually increasing difficulty while maintaining an engaging learning curve. The atmosphere is hellish, with a dark and gloomy atmosphere.

  • Created VFX, Shader Graphs, and other visual effects to enhance the game's aesthetics.
  • Implemented lighting and post-processing effects to enhance visual quality and atmosphere.
  • Optimized performance to ensure smooth gameplay on various devices.
